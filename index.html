Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions We're Here to Help

What are your operating hours?

We provide 24/7 emergency services and are always open to serve you. Our outpatient departments operate from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.

How can I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment via WhatsApp at 03055082811, call us directly, or use the contact form on our website. We respond promptly to all booking requests.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our administration desk with your insurance details for verification before your appointment.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), list of current medications, and any previous medical records relevant to your visit.

Do you provide home visit services?

Yes, we offer home visit services for patients who cannot visit the clinic. Contact us via WhatsApp or phone to schedule a home consultation.
